My Research

Research Profile

Pokpong Songmuang is a lecturer at Thammasat University, with research interests in artificial intelligence, digital competence, and data science. His work includes studies on the Bees algorithm for test construction, computerized adaptive testing, salary prediction systems for student motivation, and activity recognition using supervised learning models. He has contributed to the field through numerous publications and aims to leverage technology to solve real-world problems and enhance educational outcomes.
